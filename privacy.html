🔒 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 09, 2026

Purrrscribe is built to work entirely on your own device. This policy explains exactly what that means, plainly, without vague language — including the specific, honest exceptions where you choose to send something somewhere yourself.

This policy covers two separate things: the Purrrscribe app, and the purrrscribe.com website. They work differently, so they're explained separately below.

Part 1 — The Purrrscribe App

What the app does NOT do

The app does not collect, transmit, or store your voice recordings, transcribed text, or any content you create, anywhere outside your own device. There is no analytics tracking, no usage telemetry, and no account or sign-in required to use the app's core features. Nothing you say or write into the app is ever sent to Purrrscribe, to us, or to any third party automatically. The app has no backend servers — everything runs on your device.

How the app actually works

Speech-to-text and text-to-speech processing both happen entirely on your device, using language and voice models you choose to download once. Once a model is downloaded, that specific language or voice works completely offline — no internet connection is needed to use it.

One-time downloads

To use a language or voice, you first download its model file from our hosting (via Hugging Face). This is a one-time download per language/voice you choose — you are never required to download every available language, and downloading a model does not send any of your personal data anywhere; it only pulls a file onto your device.

Two features that DO send data somewhere — only if you choose

These are the only ways any content ever leaves your device, and both require a specific, deliberate action:

1. Share — if you tap the Share button, you choose exactly where your content goes (a messaging app, copy to clipboard, another app, etc.). This works through your device's own operating system, the same way sharing works from any app. Purrrscribe does not see or handle this content once you've shared it.

2. Cloud backup (optional) — if you choose to connect your own personal Google Drive to back up your data, that data goes directly to your own cloud storage account — not to Purrrscribe's servers, and not to us. This feature is entirely optional and off by default.

Purchases and subscriptions

Purrrscribe offers a one-time lifetime purchase, a monthly subscription, and a yearly subscription, all handled entirely through Google Play — Purrrscribe does not process payments itself and has no payment or billing servers of any kind.

One-time purchase: verified once, at the moment of purchase. After that, no further internet connection is ever required for billing purposes.

Subscriptions (monthly/yearly): require your device to periodically connect to the internet so Google Play can confirm your subscription is still active. This check happens through Google's own systems, not Purrrscribe's. If your device stays offline for an extended period, Google Play's standard grace-period handling applies.

Permissions the app requests

The app requests microphone access (required for speech-to-text) and storage access (required to save downloaded language/voice models and any files you choose to export). These permissions are used only for the stated purpose, on-device, and are never used to collect or transmit data elsewhere.

Children's privacy

Purrrscribe does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all in its normal operation. The two opt-in exceptions above (Share, Cloud backup) require the same deliberate user action regardless of age.

Part 2 — The purrrscribe.com Website

Unlike the app, the website does use cloud infrastructure for form processing and email delivery, and collects a small, specific amount of information — always something you provide directly and voluntarily. Here's exactly what, and why:

Notify-me signup (homepage)

If you enter your email address to be notified when Purrrscribe launches, that email address is stored so we can send you that one notification. It is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes, not sold, and not used for anything beyond that purpose. Your email is processed through our email delivery infrastructure solely to send you the message.

Contact form

If you send a message through the Contact page, your name, email address, and message are stored so we can read and respond to you. This information is used only to reply to your message. As with the notify signup, your email is processed through our email delivery infrastructure solely for the purpose of delivering the message.

Comments (Community pages)

Comments on the Community section are powered by GitHub Discussions (via a tool called giscus). If you choose to comment, you'll sign in with your own GitHub account, and your comment is stored and governed by GitHub's own privacy policy and terms — we don't separately collect or store your comment data ourselves.

Interactive demos (website only — not the app)

The Demos page lets you test speech-to-text and text-to-speech with your own voice or text, for a short, limited sample.

This is different from the app, and it's important to be direct about that difference: the demo runs using Hugging Face Spaces, a hosted service. When you submit audio or text to try the demo, that sample is sent to Hugging Face's infrastructure to be processed, and the result is sent back to you. This is real data transmission to a third-party service — genuinely different from the app itself, which never sends anything anywhere. This only applies to the website's demo feature.

Demo submissions are not stored or logged by Purrrscribe (the demo is built with input logging explicitly disabled), are not linked to your identity, and are not shared with anyone by us. This feature runs on Hugging Face's shared infrastructure, governed alongside Hugging Face's own privacy policy for that underlying processing.

Custom poll (Community page)

Whether you've voted, and what you picked: this is remembered only in your own browser (using local storage) — it is never sent to us, and we have no way to see it.

The overall vote counts: each vote anonymously adds one to that option's running total, stored on our servers with no name, email, or any identifying information attached — just a number going up. There's no way for us to trace a specific vote back to a specific person.

What we don't do on the website

We don't use advertising trackers, and we don't sell or share your email address or message content with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete any information the website has stored about you (your notify-me email, or a contact message) at any time by emailing support@purrrscribe.com.
